What Is Digital Menu?

A digital menu is an electronic type of menu that digitally displays the menu a restaurant, bar or lounge currently has via a digital display like your smartphone. Users can also place orders in addition to viewing your menu.

Why Choose Us

Contactless Ordering

Customers can use their own device to scan the QR code and view the menu, eliminating the need for physical menus.

Cheap

Printing and distributing physical menus can be expensive. With a digital QR menu, the restaurant can save on printing and updating costs.

Convienence

The digital menu can be easily updated in real-time, allowing the restaurant to make changes to the menu without having to print new menus.
